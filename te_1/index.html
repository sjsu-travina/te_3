What is a Wildfire?

Lets discuss the properties of a wildfire.

It is a combustion reaction caused by fire, in the wild-land/rural areas, a substance mixed with oxygen causes fire.

How is a fire formed? It needs these three elements:

  • Fuel
  • Heat
  • Oxygen

There are usually bad connotations about wildfires but not all wildfires are bad. Some forest ecosystems depend on fire. A fire regime frequently occurs after long periods of time in order for plants to survive and reproduce.
