Here are some basics on what to know about wildfires. We will talk about what its made up of and how to prevent them from happening.


When it comes to protecting communities from wildfires, firefighters can not do it alone. As our homes are surrounded by nature, it is everyones responsibility to prevent wildfires and to protect the wild. Following simple guidelines can minimize your risk of starting a wildfire and maximize your safety. Lets help our firefighters by becoming fire adaptive and Firewise.
